Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at Nanyang Technological University, advised by Prof. Tianwei Zhang and Prof. Yang Liu. I’m also working as a part-time blockchain auditor at Quantstamp.

My current research interests cover a wide ranges in cyber security domain, including system security, web security, fuzzing, blockchain and LLM security.
